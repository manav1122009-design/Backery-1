Our Story

Tradition & Taste

Founded in 2010, Sweet Crumb started with a simple mission: to bring the smell of fresh bread back to the neighborhood. We use only organic flour, local eggs, and real butter.


Every morning at 4 AM, our ovens are fired up to ensure that when you walk through our doors, everything is fresh and ready for you.
